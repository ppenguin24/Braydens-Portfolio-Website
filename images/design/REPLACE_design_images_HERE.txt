Notes:
- Use JPG by default. For PNG, open index.html and change the
  extension from .jpg to .png on both the <a href="..."> and the
  <img src="..."> for that tile.
- For a PDF, save it here (e.g. 01.pdf) and edit index.html to change
  the <a href="images/design/01.jpg"> to <a href="images/design/01.pdf">.
- Aspect ratio: tiles display 4:3, so landscape crops look cleanest.
- To change the caption under a tile, edit the <span class="tile-label">
  text in index.html.
